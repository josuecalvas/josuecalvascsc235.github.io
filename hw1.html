The Genius of Tyler, the Creator


 Tyler, The Creator by Luis 'Panch' Perez for Robb Report

Who is he?

Starting off as a teen in the late 2000's, Tyler, The Creator is not the same artist he was when he first joined the underground scene; with the founding of the collective known as Odd Future [1] . The now 31 year old is now a two-time grammy winner and a highly respected artist who has been shattering the glass ceiling time and time again. Whether it be through fashion, music, or fragrances Tyler, the Creator is not a force to be reckoned with.


Fashion?

“Don't be scared of color.”

Since he first launched into the world of clothing with his brand OFWGKTA [2] has changed the game multiple times; whether it be with his popularization of the box logo tee or the craze over anything donut related; Tyler seems to know what people will like. More recently though, he has been creating an entirely different game. The once donut logo sporting artist, has stepped into the world of luxury, popularizing the classic old money look, with his own spin on it. With loafers, and cardigans Tyler has created an empire based on the 80's look of LaCoste and other luxury brands, reviving the love of classics to a fashion world over-saturated with 'hype'. Always focused on working for himself instead of the middle-man, Tyler has built his own business with his vision, when the world tells him 1+1=2 he overcomes expectations and denies the laws of math.
